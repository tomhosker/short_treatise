The ancient history of the Honey Monsters is both sparse and contradictory. The ancient text of the Codex Melifluus speaks of the sorcerer Rereggub'taog, who stirred various savage ingredients into one cauldron - the claws of the tiger, the bite of the crocodile, the hide of the elephant, the cunning of the sedge warbler - and then, calling on dark gods and entering into unspeakable pacts with several Lovecraftian entities of fearsome renown, combined them into one creature.
